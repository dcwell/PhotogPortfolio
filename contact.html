About Me

My name is Denali Cornwell, I am a professional photographer, university student, and musician based in Seattle, WA. I mainly focus on portrait photography, but I love to explore other types of photography as well. Complementary to my photography skills, my experience in the engineering department at my university has allowed me to become very detailed oriented in my photography, and I absolutely love capturing the finer details and character of what I am photographing.
